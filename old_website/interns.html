TASA Internship program

pGet firsthand experience working with TASA staff and learn about the behind-the-scenes of your favorite TASA events!

why become a tasa intern?

Interning with TASA is the easiest way to explore roles and responsibilities within TASA staff while also contributing back to the club's community and members! Whether you're interested in running for a full-year staff position or simply passionate about TASA and eager to help out, here's why you should consider applying to become a TASA intern! 

Learn about
​leadership

Find out if working on TASA staff would be the right fit for you! See how TASA events are run and organize your own events/programs

Give back to the community

Help improve and revitalize TASA events for current and future members. Be a part of the welcoming space that TASA offers

Make friends and connections

Bond with your fellow interns and learn from staff mentors. Put yourself out there as a representative of TASA
Check out past events our interns have planned and organized as teams!
